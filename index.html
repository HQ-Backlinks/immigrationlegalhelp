Our Services

At Immigration Legal Help, we offer a broad range of services to meet all of your U.S. immigration needs:

1. Visa Assistance

Whether you're applying for a work visa, student visa, or tourist visa, we provide expert legal guidance on the visa application process. Our team helps ensure that you meet the eligibility requirements, submit the correct documentation, and avoid common pitfalls that could delay your application.

  • Work Visas: Including H-1B, L-1, and O-1 visas.

  • Student Visas: F-1 and M-1 visa guidance for international students.

  • Tourist Visas: Assistance with B-1 and B-2 visitor visas.

2. Green Cards (Permanent Residency)

Obtaining a green card is a major step toward establishing permanent residency in the U.S. We assist individuals and families in applying for green cards through various channels, including family sponsorship, employment-based petitions, and more. Whether you're applying from outside the U.S. or adjusting your status while inside the country, we handle all aspects of the process.

  • Family-Based Green Cards: For spouses, children, parents, and siblings of U.S. citizens or permanent residents.

  • Employment-Based Green Cards: For professionals, skilled workers, and other eligible applicants.

  • Adjustment of Status: For those already in the U.S. on a valid visa.

3. Citizenship and Naturalization

Becoming a U.S. citizen is a proud milestone in any immigrant s journey. We offer full support for individuals looking to apply for U.S. citizenship through naturalization. Our legal experts help you meet the eligibility requirements, prepare for your citizenship interview, and guide you through the entire naturalization process.

  • Eligibility Review: Assess whether you meet the legal requirements for citizenship.

  • Application Support: Complete and submit all necessary forms.

  • Interview Preparation: Help you prepare for the U.S. citizenship interview and civics test.

4. Family Reunification

We understand the importance of family and are committed to helping families reunite through the U.S. immigration system. Our team assists with family-based visa petitions, including spousal visas, fiance visas, and visas for children and parents. We handle the legal complexities to make the process as smooth as possible for you and your loved ones.

  • Spousal and Fiance Visas: K-1 and K-3 visa petitions for partners of U.S. citizens.

  • Parent and Child Sponsorship: Family-based immigration petitions for immediate relatives.

5. Legal Support for Complex Immigration Issues

U.S. immigration law can be complicated, and some cases involve unique challenges, such as visa denials, deportation proceedings, or requests for additional evidence (RFE) from USCIS. Our legal team at Immigration Legal Help provides expert support for these complex matters, helping you overcome obstacles and move forward with your immigration plans.

  • Deportation Defense: Legal representation to protect your rights and fight deportation.

  • Appeals and Waivers: Assistance with immigration appeals and waiver applications.

  • Requests for Evidence (RFE): Help in responding to USCIS requests for additional documentation.
